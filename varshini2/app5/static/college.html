ACS Medical College and Hospital is a premier medical institution in Chennai, India. It is part of the Dr. MGR Educational and Research Institute, a deemed-to-be university. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in medicine and allied health sciences, focusing on high-quality education, research, and clinical training. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a commitment to holistic healthcare, ACS Medical College aims to produce skilled medical professionals to serve society effectively.
