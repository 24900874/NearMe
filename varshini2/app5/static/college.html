The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University

Chennai's Top Government University

Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, officially known as The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, is one of the premier medical institutions in India, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1987, the university is named after the former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, M.G. Ramachandran (MGR), who was a prominent leader and philanthropist. It was set up with the goal of providing high-quality education in the field of medicine, and it has since grown to become one of the leading medical universities in the country.
